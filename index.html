    About Me


    YOU ARE IN A FRONT END DEV PROGRAM, SO WHY GAME DEV PROJECTS?

    On previous Udacity FEND projects I took it safe and easy as to be sure I made as little mistakes as possible. With this being the last project before I graduate I've decided to take a risk and go big! Also with this being my first portfolio project I wanted to display as much as my skills as possible. Another reason I've decided to show of my game dev projects instead of web developement is because, believe it or not, I've never had anyone try any of my games so this is also a chance to get some feedback on what I can improve upon and hopefully come out with the next best thing!


    WHAT'S NEXT?

    FINDING A JOB! Haha just kidding. But no really the whole reason I applied to the FEND scholarship is to shift gears career wise into a field that's growing exponentially. So my future goals are to add at least one game a month to this page as to keep honing both my web development skills as well as my game dev skills. And once I finally land that developement job I plan to release one of the video games here to the app store as my final 'leap of faith'. I can't wait to show you all my hard work and I am excited for what the future holds.
